Privacy Policy for YouTube Timestamp Bookmarker

Last Updated: November 15, 2024

Introduction

YouTube Timestamp Bookmarker ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

This extension operates entirely locally on your device. All data is stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API and never leaves your computer.

What Data is Stored Locally

The extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Video bookmarks: Timestamps you bookmark in YouTube videos
  • Bookmark notes: Optional notes you add to bookmarks
  • Video titles: Titles of videos you have bookmarked
  • Video IDs: YouTube video IDs for organizing bookmarks

This data is stored in Chrome's local storage on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Permissions Used

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your bookmarks locally on your device
  • ActiveTab: To access the current YouTube video page when you click the extension icon
  • Scripting: To inject the bookmark button into YouTube pages
  • Host Permissions (youtube.com): To access YouTube video pages and add bookmark functionality

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Remote Code

This extension does NOT use remote code. All JavaScript code is included in the extension package and runs locally.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • You can delete bookmarks at any time through the extension popup
  • You can uninstall the extension, which will remove all stored bookmarks
  • All data is stored in Chrome's local storage, which you can clear through Chrome settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
